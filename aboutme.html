About Me


Welcome to the Curry Girl Adventures

Well Hello There! My name is Amanda, i'm a blogger, wife, dog mommy, and a foodie/travel adventurist. It that entices you, then please make yourself comfy and stick around for the ride

In the fall of 2015, we had opportunity knocking on our door. It was a chance that we couldn't pass up. So we packed up our things and left Seattle for the Rockies.


Our lives have been busy with exploring Denver, and the surrounding areas, including the food scene which we are still on the look out for our favorite places.

My husband Som doesn't feature much on this site and that is his choice, not mine. He is anti social media and I still shove a camera in his face when I can.
